# Insight Bank — Torq

## Keeper stories

### 1. Name collision = identity death
**Observation:** A peer submission used near-identical naming.  
**Interpretation:** Judges assume copying on second glance.  
**Recommendation:** Torq locked; keep old working titles out of UI/docs; state differentiation in submission without trash-talk.

### 2. Score ≠ felt game
**Observation:** Fans already have scores; the dense signal they lack is market movement.  
**Interpretation:** Wave is the product; scores are table stakes.  
**Recommendation:** First viewport and demo must lead with the river, not the lobby chrome.

### 3. Replay is the judge insurance
**Observation:** Review happens after whistle / after tournament.  
**Interpretation:** Live-only demos die on Monday.  
**Recommendation:** `?replay=1` + materialised history is P0 infrastructure, not a nice-to-have.

### 4. Integrate Solana, don’t invent DeFi
**Observation:** TxLINE already owns subscribe + Merkle validation.  
**Interpretation:** Building vaults/AMMs is wrong track and wrong risk.  
**Recommendation:** Keep wallet optional identity; proofs read-only.
